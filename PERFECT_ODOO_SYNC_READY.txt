🆘 QUICK TROUBLESHOOTING:
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Problem: Connection refused
Solution: Verify ODOO_URL, check Odoo is running, test network

Problem: Authentication failed
Solution: Verify ODOO_USERNAME and ODOO_PASSWORD, check user exists

Problem: Sync fails silently
Solution: Check Celery is running, view sync events in admin, check logs

Problem: Lock conflicts
Solution: Wait 5 minutes or run SyncLock.release_all_expired()

Problem: Data mismatch
Solution: Review SyncConflict in admin, auto-resolve or manually merge
